Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Naga Gospel Songs ("we," "our," or "the App") handles information when you access and use the application. The App is designed primarily to provide offline access to hymn lyrics and titles. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

02

Information Collection and Use

The App itself does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, the App incorporates a third-party developer tool that may collect information automatically. Specifically, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. To learn more about how Google handles your data, please review the Google Privacy & Terms.

03

Third-Party Data Collection (Google AdMob)

Google AdMob may automatically collect and process certain data to serve relevant ads, limit ad repetition, and detect fraud. This data includes:

  • Device identifiers: advertising IDs (such as Android Advertising ID), device models, and operating system versions.
  • Network & location data: IP addresses, mobile carrier details, and approximate location data.
  • Interaction data: information about how you interact with the advertisements shown within the App.
04

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data to third parties. Any user data processing is handled strictly by our third-party advertising partner (Google AdMob) as disclosed above.

Any sharing of hymn text or titles occurs solely through your own user-initiated actions — for example, using your phone's built-in system share menu to copy text or send it via external messaging or social media apps. The App does not track or store the content you choose to share.

05

Offline Functionality

The core features of the App function entirely offline. An active internet connection is only used to load and display advertisements via Google AdMob.

06

Data Security

We care about protecting your privacy. While the App does not store personal data on external servers, our third-party provider, Google AdMob, implements industry-standard encryption and security protocols to safeguard the device information it processes.

07

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all audiences but is not explicitly targeted at children under the age of 13. Google AdMob complies with applicable regulatory standards for child-directed ad delivery where configured in the developer settings.

08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date at the top. We recommend checking this page periodically for updates.

09
